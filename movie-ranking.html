The Best Movies According to Jordan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

This is a great movie about a man that is betrayed by an army general but seeks revenge.

The Notebook

This a romantic movie about love. The love of a poor guy and a rich woman, but the one thing that brings them together is love.

Friday

This is a comedic movie which showcases two friends. One of the friends got fired on his day off and the neighborhood bully tries to take everything from the residents.
